How it works (and what it isn’t)

    Self-computed strength

    A Bayesian goal model

    Model M1 — a dynamic hierarchical Poisson fit with NUTS — turns ratings into goal expectations with full uncertainty.

    We play the whole World Cup twenty-thousand times over, group stage to final, and count how often each team ends up lifting it.
